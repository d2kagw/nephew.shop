I’ll be honest, I’m jealous of my nephew’s cool clothes.

How come kids have all these amazing clothes with cool designs featuring giant robots battling or dinosaurs with lasers. Meanwhile, adults like me are stuck with boring, plain “natural tone basics”!?

I’m sitting here in my 40s wondering where the fuck my t-shirt of a giant robot battling a dinosaur is!?

And so, the idea for Nephew was born.
A menswear brand that captures the fun and style of kids’ clothes while still being stylish and wearable for adults - with influences of 80s pop culture, geekdom, and cyberpunk.

Our first drop is in development.
